Extension Privacy Policy


Last updated: 22 August 2022

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when using the hulio extension and the choices you have associated with that data.

We use your data to provide and improve the service. By using the service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

“Extension” refers to our browser extension, which you can download from the Chrome Web Store.

“The hulio website” refers to https://hulio.app

Summary

  • By default, we do not track extension usage.

    Extension usage is only tracked if users opt in to receive rewards, and in that case the only information tracked is what tutorials have been completed.

  • We never send extension settings and of your browser.

    Extension settings are stored locally.

  • By default, the extension interacts with the websites that we have verified and websites that we detect might be crypto and Solana related.

    The extension also interacts with websites that we have made tutorials for, however these tutorials are opt-in.

Permanently-Stored Information

We store some data locally on your device permanently. This information is still available even if your browser is restarted.

This data can only be accessed by the hulio extension on your browser.

Permanently-stored information might include:

  • Extension settings (what features are enabled and their settings).
  • Database of verified websites and their corresponding transaction hashes on the Solana Blockchain
  • Database of unsafe websites
  • Lists of user ignored websites

The extension by itself does not send any of this information outside of your device.

Third Parties

By default, Scratch Addons only reaches 2 services automatically:

  1. hulio api
  1. The hulio github

Some extension features like tutorials will require you to provide your phone number to a third party in order to ensure people do not abuse the rewards system.

Other Software

In case you use other extensions in your browser that can interact with the hulio extension these extensions might be able to know everything that the Scratch website itself could potentially know.

Any malware running in your device could also potentially read any data stored by the hulio extension.

Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us through our feedback page at team@hulio (dot) app
